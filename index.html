Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the privacy terms of our application "MTProto Light for TG". Our application is a lightweight technical tool designed to help users find and monitor MTProto proxy addresses with a single click. It provides real-time status checks, response time measurements, and quick address discovery for Telegram MTProto protocol.

CORE DISCLAIMER: This application is NOT a VPN provider. We do not provide tunnel services, do not reroute your device traffic, and do not assist in bypassing regional restrictions. The application is strictly for informational purposes — it helps you discover and monitor MTProto addresses in one click, but does not establish any VPN connections or proxy tunnels itself.

2. Information Collection and Use

We prioritize user privacy. We do not require account registration, and we do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2.1. Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics

To ensure the app runs smoothly, we use Firebase services to collect anonymous data about technical errors and general usage trends. This helps us improve stability and user experience. Firebase services are provided by Google LLC. For more information on how Firebase handles your data, please refer to the Firebase Privacy Policy and Google Privacy Policy.

2.2. Advertising (Yandex Advertising Network / РСЯ)

We use the Yandex Advertising Network (Рекламная Сеть Яндекса, РСЯ) to display advertisements. To provide relevant ads, the SDK may collect pseudonymous identifiers (such as Advertising ID), IP addresses, and approximate location data. This data is handled in accordance with the Yandex Privacy Policy (and its Mobile Ads appendix). You can learn more about how Yandex uses data for ad personalization and how to opt out by visiting the official Yandex Ad Privacy documentation.

3. Permissions

  • Internet Access: To fetch status and availability of MTProto addresses.
  • Network State: To check connectivity before performing tests.
  • AD_ID Permission: To provide personalized advertising and analytics.

4. Data Deletion and Retention

How to request data deletion:

Since we do not use accounts, most collected data (like crash logs) is anonymous. However, if you wish to request the deletion of any associated technical identifiers, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to boardgames.bd@gmail.com.
  2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line.
  3. We will process your request and ensure that any associated technical logs are purged from our analytics systems within 30 days.

Note: Most analytics data is automatically deleted from our servers every 90 days.
